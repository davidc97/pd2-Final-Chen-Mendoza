pd2-Final-Chen-Mendoza
======================

APCS Final Project Pd 2 Spring Term

Project: A single player game where the user captures and battles with creatures using different items.
The battling system is turn based and the creatures may either restore health, deal damage, or inflict status conditions.

Plot: A young boy is disowned by his parents for being a failure. 
He is told that he can not come back until he regains the family grail and restores his and his family's honor.
